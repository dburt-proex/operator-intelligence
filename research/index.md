# Operator Intelligence Research Index

Version: v1.0 commercial research registry  
Stage alignment: Stage 8 — `research/`  
Folder alignment: `research/`  
Status: Canonical support-research registry

## 1. Purpose

Register the research protocols permitted to support commercial v1.0 assessment work. Research adds bounded context and validation support; it does not replace client evidence, scoring rules, or recommendation gates.

## 2. Registry

| Protocol | Permitted support |
|---|---|
| `market-research-protocol.md` | Market context, service/buyer framing, strategic-fit questions |
| `competitor-research-protocol.md` | Defined comparable-set public observations |
| `conversion-research-protocol.md` | Friction mechanisms, test design, and acceptance criteria |
| `seo-research-protocol.md` | Official search requirements, technical validation, and bounded SEO context |

## 3. Shared rules

- primary and authoritative sources take priority
- source, date, method, geography, definitions, sample, and limitations are recorded
- research observations remain separate from client facts
- public or modeled data does not create internal conclusions
- research cannot independently create a negative finding or package route
- industry averages do not become client baselines
- appearance does not establish competitor performance
- external uplift does not become a client forecast
- keyword volume does not establish demand, capacity, or revenue
- material research decisions are evidence-registered and ledgered

## 4. Use sequence

```text
Client decision question
→ Bounded research question
→ Source hierarchy
→ Research object
→ Applicability review
→ Evidence admission
→ Limited client interpretation
→ Decision / validation support
```

## 5. Commercial v1.0 connection

This registry provides controlled support for the four research domains required by the release while preventing speculative market, competitor, conversion, and SEO claims.
