# Contractor Base Playbook

This playbook applies to most local service contractors.

## Common buyer questions

- Do they serve my area?
- Do they do the specific work I need?
- Are they trustworthy?
- Are they licensed or insured?
- What will it cost?
- How soon can they help?
- What does the process look like?
- What proof do they have?
- How do I request an estimate?

## Universal required assets

- Clear homepage
- Service pages
- Contact page
- Google Business Profile
- Reviews
- Project proof
- Mobile-friendly estimate form
- Click-to-call phone number
- Service area language
- Local SEO foundation

## Universal conversion improvements

- Strong primary CTA
- Tap-to-call on mobile
- Estimate form
- Response-time expectation
- Project detail fields
- Review highlights
- Trust proof near CTA
- Follow-up automation

## Universal automation opportunities

- Inquiry capture
- Internal notification
- CRM pipeline
- Estimate reminders
- Follow-up sequence
- Review request sequence
- Monthly performance dashboard

## Universal reporting metrics

- Leads by source
- Estimate requests
- Phone calls
- Form submissions
- Review count
- Review velocity
- Website traffic
- Search queries
- Service page performance
- Close rate if available

## Universal red flags

- No clear CTA
- No service pages
- Few reviews
- No project proof
- No mobile optimization
- No tracking
- No CRM
- No follow-up system
- No clear differentiation
