# Changelog

All notable changes to Operator Intelligence are documented here.

## v0.1.0 — Foundation

### Added

- Core repository architecture.
- Product vision and operating principles.
- Business Growth Systems Assessment methodology.
- Operator Score model.
- Scoring weights and maturity tiers.
- Evidence standard and confidence model.
- Standards library for website, SEO, Google Business Profile, conversion, trust, automation, AI readiness, analytics, and reporting.
- Executive report template.
- Contractor-specific report template.
- Initial industry playbooks for painting, tree service, roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, and remodeling.

### Purpose

v0.1 establishes Operator Intelligence as a governed consulting methodology, not a one-off audit template.

### Next

v0.2 will expand scoring into a full criteria library with 100+ observable signals and recommendation mappings.
