## 1. Purpose

Use this report as the concise operator-facing companion to the executive report. It translates governed evidence, scores, findings, recommendations, packages, and roadmap decisions into practical language for contractor and local-service owners.

The contractor report must remain consistent with the same source objects and publication decision as the executive report. It is not a simplified license to omit limitations or authorization boundaries.
