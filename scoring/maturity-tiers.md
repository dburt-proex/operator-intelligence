# Maturity Tiers

## Tier 1 — Foundational Risk

Score range: 0–39

The business may have individual assets, but the growth system is weak, inconsistent, or mostly manual.

Common signals:

- Outdated or missing website
- Weak Google presence
- Few reviews
- Poor service clarity
- No CRM
- No follow-up process
- No reporting
- No structured lead handling

Recommended action:

Establish core digital trust, lead capture, and response infrastructure before scaling marketing spend.

## Tier 2 — Developing

Score range: 40–59

The business has some useful assets, but the customer journey leaks opportunity.

Common signals:

- Website exists but has weak conversion
- Some SEO foundation but missing service pages
- Reviews exist but no review system
- Manual lead tracking
- Limited analytics
- Inconsistent social proof

Recommended action:

Fix conversion constraints, strengthen local SEO, and install basic lead management.

## Tier 3 — Growth Ready

Score range: 60–74

The business has credible infrastructure and can benefit from optimization.

Common signals:

- Professional website
- Basic service pages
- Active Google Business Profile
- Decent review base
- Clear contact path
- Some reporting
- Some repeatable workflows

Recommended action:

Optimize conversion, expand SEO coverage, improve reputation systems, and introduce automation.

## Tier 4 — High Performing

Score range: 75–89

The business has a strong growth system but still has opportunities for compounding advantage.

Common signals:

- Strong website and messaging
- Clear conversion paths
- Strong reputation
- Competitive search visibility
- CRM or pipeline process
- Automated follow-up
- Reporting cadence

Recommended action:

Build advanced content, deeper automation, AI support, retention systems, and competitive monitoring.

## Tier 5 — Market Leader

Score range: 90–100

The business demonstrates durable competitive advantage across visibility, trust, conversion, operations, and measurement.

Common signals:

- Strong local rankings
- High review velocity
- High-quality proof assets
- Mature CRM
- Strong follow-up system
- Clear dashboards
- Automated customer lifecycle
- Excellent offer clarity
- Distinct positioning

Recommended action:

Protect advantage through ongoing optimization, brand authority, operational governance, and market expansion.
