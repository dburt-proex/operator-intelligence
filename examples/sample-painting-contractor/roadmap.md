## 4. Phase 0 — Validation and Access

### Objective

Resolve workflow ownership, measurement definitions, form routing, and AI control gaps without treating validation as implementation.

### Exit criteria

- current-state inquiry workflow approved
- source/stage/next-action fields defined
- form destination and owner verified
- metric/source map approved
- AI blockers either resolved through a separate control program or remain halted

### Governance rule

No Phase 0 item carries a primary package or implementation authorization.

## 5. Phase 1 — Quick Wins

### Authorized candidate

`OI-REC-2026-EX001` may become implementation-ready after:

- form-routing prerequisite passes
- `OI-PKG-WEB-001` scope is accepted
- access and rollback are verified
- implementation authorization is issued

### Completion evidence

- desktop and mobile form screenshots
- approved field map
- validation/error/confirmation results
- safe submission received by approved owner
- no unnecessary or sensitive fields introduced

## 6. Phase 2 — Growth Foundation

### Cabinet service path

Create only the verified cabinet-painting page and approved internal links. No ranking guarantee or unsupported durability/warranty claim.

### Project proof

Structure selected authentic examples and connect them to applicable services. No fabricated results or permission assumptions.

### Dependency logic

Cabinet proof placement may depend on the cabinet-page structure, but the SEO and Trust packages retain separate primary ownership.

## 7. Phase 3 — Automation and Reporting

CRM and dashboard work remain ineligible until Phase 0 establishes:

- workflow stages and ownership
- required fields and exceptions
- source and outcome definitions
- data authority and quality limitations
- package-specific acceptance criteria

Priority 89 does not allow the workflow recommendation to bypass validation.

- no relevant HALT applies

## 11. Change control

Any material change to findings, priority, eligibility, package ownership, phase, scope, timing assumption, authorization, or acceptance evidence requires a superseding roadmap and DecisionLedger event.
